What are affiliate links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track when you click through from our site to a tool's website. If you make a purchase, we may earn a small commission. This never costs you extra — in fact, our deals often save you money.

How do I use a coupon code?

Click "Get Deal" to go to the tool's website. During checkout, look for a field labeled "Coupon Code," "Promo Code," or "Discount Code." Paste the code and click Apply. The discount should appear in your order summary.
