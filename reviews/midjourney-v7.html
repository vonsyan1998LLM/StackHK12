Midjourney v7 Review

The Best AI Image Generator?
Image & Video TRENDING Published May 5, 2025 · Updated May 10, 2025

Quick Verdict

Midjourney v7 is the best AI image generator for quality and aesthetic consistency. Its photorealistic output is stunning, and the new web editor makes it more accessible than ever. If you create visual content professionally, the $10/month Basic plan is an incredible value.

What is Midjourney?

Midjourney is an AI image generation service that creates stunning visuals from text descriptions. Version 7, released in early 2025, represents a massive leap in photorealism, coherence, and text rendering within images.

Originally accessible only through Discord, Midjourney now offers a full web-based editor with drag-and-drop canvas, image editing tools, and a more intuitive interface. This makes it accessible to users who never touched Discord.

Our Testing Process

We tested Midjourney v7 extensively across different use cases:

  • Product photography mockups for e-commerce
  • Blog post featured images and social media graphics
  • Architectural visualization and interior design concepts
  • Character design for game development
  • Logo concepts and brand identity exploration
  • Photo editing and style transfer experiments

Image Quality

Midjourney v7 produces the most aesthetically pleasing images of any AI generator we've tested. While DALL-E 3 is more prompt-accurate and Stable Diffusion is more customizable, Midjourney's output has a "wow factor" that's hard to match.

"v7's photorealism is genuinely impressive. We generated product photos that were indistinguishable from professional studio shots. For content creators and marketers, this is a game-changer."

The new text rendering capabilities are a significant improvement. While not perfect, v7 can reliably render short text strings within images — something that was nearly impossible in earlier versions.

Key Features

Web Editor

The new web-based editor is a massive upgrade over the Discord-only workflow. It includes a canvas for arranging and editing generations, inpainting tools, and a gallery for organizing your work. This alone makes v7 worth the upgrade.

Style References

Upload reference images to guide Midjourney's aesthetic. This is incredibly useful for maintaining brand consistency or matching a specific visual style across multiple generations.

Zoom & Pan

Expand any image beyond its original boundaries with intelligent zoom and pan. This lets you iterate on compositions without starting from scratch.

Character Consistency

v7 introduces better character consistency, allowing you to maintain the same character across multiple images. This is invaluable for storytelling, comics, or brand mascots.

Pricing

Midjourney offers several tiers:

  • Basic: $10/month — 200 generations, 3 concurrent jobs
  • Standard: $30/month — Unlimited generations, 15 concurrent jobs
  • Pro: $60/month — Unlimited + stealth mode, 30 concurrent jobs
  • Mega: $120/month — Everything + 60 concurrent jobs

No free tier is available, which is a notable downside. However, the Basic plan at $10/month offers excellent value for casual users.

While DALL-E 3 is more prompt-accurate and Stable Diffusion offers more control, Midjourney hits the sweet spot of quality, ease of use, and value. The $10/month Basic plan is one of the best bargains in AI tools.
