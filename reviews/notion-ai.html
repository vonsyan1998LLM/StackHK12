Notion AI Review

Your All-in-One Workspace Gets Smarter
Productivity Published May 10, 2025

Quick Verdict

Notion AI seamlessly integrates artificial intelligence into the workspace millions already use. It's not the most powerful AI writer, but its deep integration with your existing notes, databases, and wikis makes it incredibly practical for teams already in the Notion ecosystem.

What is Notion AI?

Notion AI is an add-on to the popular Notion productivity platform that brings AI-powered writing, summarization, translation, and Q&A capabilities directly into your workspace. Unlike standalone AI tools, Notion AI works within the context of your existing documents and databases.

The key differentiator is context — Notion AI can search across your entire workspace to answer questions, summarize meeting notes, or draft content based on your existing knowledge base.

Our Testing Process

We used Notion AI daily for 2 weeks across various workflows:

  • Drafting project documentation and wikis
  • Summarizing meeting notes and action items
  • Writing blog posts and marketing copy
  • Q&A across our knowledge base
  • Translating content for international teams
  • Generating database views and formulas

AI Writing Quality

Notion AI's writing capabilities are solid but not best-in-class. For short-form content like meeting summaries, email drafts, and bullet point expansions, it performs admirably. However, for long-form articles or creative writing, dedicated tools like Claude or ChatGPT produce noticeably better results.

"Where Notion AI shines is not raw writing quality, but contextual awareness. It knows your team's style, your project's history, and your document's structure."

Key Features

AI Q&A

The Q&A feature lets you ask questions in natural language and get answers drawn from your entire Notion workspace. It's like having a search engine that actually understands your content. We found it especially useful for onboarding new team members who need to quickly find information across dozens of documents.

Auto-Fill Properties

For database users, Notion AI can automatically fill in properties based on page content. This is a huge time saver for CRM-style databases, project trackers, and content calendars.

Inline AI Commands

Highlight any text and access AI commands: summarize, explain, translate, improve writing, change tone, and more. The inline nature means you never leave your writing flow.

Pricing

Notion AI is available as an add-on to any Notion plan:

  • Plus AI: $10/member/month (added to Plus plan)
  • Business AI: $18/member/month (added to Business plan)
  • Free tier: Limited AI responses per month

For teams already paying for Notion, the AI add-on is reasonably priced. Standalone, it's harder to justify versus dedicated AI tools.

Who Should Use Notion AI?

✅ Best For

  • Teams already using Notion as their workspace
  • Content-heavy organizations with large wikis
  • Project managers needing quick summaries
  • International teams needing translation

❌ Not Ideal For

  • Users who don't already use Notion
  • Writers needing best-in-class AI output
  • Solo users on a tight budget
  • Teams needing specialized AI (code, images)

Final Verdict

Notion AI is the best AI assistant for teams already living in Notion. It won't replace dedicated AI writing tools, but its deep workspace integration creates a unique value proposition. If Notion is your team's home base, the AI add-on is a no-brainer productivity boost.
