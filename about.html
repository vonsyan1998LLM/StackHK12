About StackHK

Hong Kong's premier AI tool review media. Tested by humans, not marketing teams.

Who We Are

StackHK is a Hong Kong-based media team dedicated to honest, in-depth reviews of AI tools and products.

We cut through the hype. Every week, we put the latest AI software to the test — covering productivity tools, creative apps, writing assistants, coding tools, and more — and give you a clear, practical verdict on what's actually worth your time and money.

Our content is built for professionals, founders, and curious minds across Asia and beyond who want to stay ahead of the AI curve without spending hours researching on their own.

Our Mission

In a world flooded with AI tool marketing and paid promotions, we provide something rare: trustworthy, independent reviews that help you make informed decisions.

We believe that:

  • Readers deserve transparency about how we test and how we make money

Our Review Methodology

We take our reviews seriously. Here's how we test every AI tool that appears on StackHK:

⏱️ Minimum 2 Weeks Testing

We spend at least 2 weeks using each tool for real work tasks before publishing a review. No quick first impressions — only sustained, practical experience.

📊 Structured Scoring

Our scores are based on 5 dimensions: Features, Value for Money, User Experience, Output Quality, and Support & Documentation.

🎯 Real-World Tasks

We test tools with actual work scenarios — writing blog posts, coding projects, analyzing data, creating images — not synthetic benchmarks.

🔄 Regular Updates

AI tools evolve rapidly. We update our reviews when significant changes occur, and revisit top picks quarterly.

How We Make Money

Transparency is core to our mission. Here's how StackHK generates revenue:

  • Affiliate Links: Some links on our site are affiliate links. When you click through and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.
  • Sponsored Content: Occasionally, we publish clearly labeled sponsored content. This never influences our independent reviews.
  • Newsletter Advertising: Our weekly newsletter may include sponsored messages, always clearly identified.

Our commitment: Affiliate commissions never influence our ratings, rankings, or editorial opinions. We never accept payment for positive reviews. Our loyalty is to our readers, not to any tool vendor.
